FAQ

Questions we actually get asked

What happens when I have more than 3 active projects on the free plan?
You can archive completed projects to stay under the limit, or upgrade to Pro for unlimited. We won't lock you out of your data either way.
Does Dispatch take a percentage of my invoices?
No. You pay a flat monthly fee. Your revenue is yours. Payment processing fees (if you use Stripe through Dispatch) are Stripe's standard rate — we don't add to it.
Do my clients need to create an account?
No. Clients get a link. They can view the project, leave feedback, sign contracts, and pay — all without signing up. We designed it this way because getting clients to create accounts kills adoption.
Can I bring in projects I'm already working on?
Yes. You can create projects for work that's already in progress. Import a past proposal, set a start date that's already passed, and add existing files and conversations.
Is there a mobile app?
Not yet. Dispatch is web-first and optimized for mobile browsers. A native app is on the roadmap — but we wanted to get the core workflow right first before building a second interface.
